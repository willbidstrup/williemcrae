Inferno

We are playing the Inferno campaign, using a modified version of the 5th Edition Dungeons & Dragons rules.
You have twelve sessions to escpape from Hell.

Session 1

The Cast:
  • Lucrazia, atoning for the sin of "Theft", taking the form of The Serpent.
  • Andre, atoning for the sin of "Heresy", taking the form of The Heresiarch.
  • Alessia, atoning for the sin of "Violence Against Oneself", taking the form of The Jester.
  • Leonardo, atoning for the sin of "Violence Against God", taking the form of The Saint.
  • Brother William Of The Abyss, a spirit of unknown origins, acting as The Guide.

You awakened in The Dark Forest, and did battle with The First Keepers Of Hell.
Lucrazia attempted to deceive The Lion by playing dead, and she was helped to make her performance very convicing.
One by one, you were defeated by the beasts, ripped and voided, until you woke again on the shore of a glowing pool, where you met The Guide .
He led you through The Eternal Gate into a realm of endless grey, where the Indifferent Damned receive their punishment for failing to choose sides in the Divine War.

Lucrazia met Marguerite The Weaver.
You all met Malaphar The Cruel and made a bargain with him to behead an Indifferent Angel by the name of Sariel in exchange for passage across the Acheronte.
You followed through on your bargain, and the spear strikes of Leonardo and spellcasting from Alessia were decisive in the battle.

Session 2

The Cast:
  • Lucrazia, atoning for the sin of "Theft", taking the form of The Serpent.
  • Leonardo, atoning for the sin of "Violence Against God", taking the form of The Saint.
  • Borgas, atoning for the sin of "Wrath", taking the form of The Beast.
  • Giancamo, atoning for the sin of "Hypocrisy", taking the form of The Vicar.
  • Brother William Of The Abyss, a Noble Spirit.

You presented the severed head of Sariel The Indifferent Angel to Malaphar The Cruel, and thus gained passage on the ferry across The Acheronte.
You were beset by hordes of Indifferent Damned, yet you prevailed and stopped the Torch Of Divine Flame from being lost forever.
With the insults of Caronte still ringing in your ears, you stepped foot on the rocky and ashen land of Limbo, also known as The First Circle Of Hell. This was a barren and haunting landscape, filled with mazes and stones depicting blasphemous scenes of old pagan gods.
You navigated the obelisks and made your way towards The Castle Of The Noble Spirits. You did battle with the foul devil Mistral The Devourer, and rescued the soul of the boy Icaro, exchanging Hope so you might baptize him in the crystal clear waters surrounding the castle.
You each made a deal with a Noble Spirit in exchange for one of their golden ribs, which have been used to create a powerful Lantern Of The Divine Flame. This will help you as you continue your quest...
