Willie McRae... in "High Stakes"

High Stakes was a hard rock band, active from 1998 to 2006.
We played hard for about eight years.
Unfortunately, I've lost the original domain for the High Stakes website.
Also, we never formally released most of our music.
So for now, this page is the best place to go to hear us.
In the future, we will set up a dedicated space to carry all these memories and music.
If you have any recordings, photos, or memories you'd like to share, please send me an email.

  • Tom Ireland (vocals)
  • Willie McRae (guitar)
  • Roddy Rod (drums)
  • Scotty Diss (guitar)
  • Angus Wallace (bass, 1998)
  • Andrew "Winz" Winzor (bass, 1998-2000)
