I programmed the drums (using Fruity Loops, I think) and laid down the voices and guitars in our house in Yeerongpilly.
I was probably thinking a lot about The Phantom to begin with, and things take a kind of Cthulhu-ish turn at the end…
I play a rocked up version live sometimes.

Space Trucker (When I Get To Where I'm Going)

This was written and released as “When I Get To Where I’m Going” but almost everyone who hears it thinks “Space Trucker” or “Moon Trucker” is a better title, so I’m changing it.
I was channeling ZZ Top with the synth bass sounds and lead guitar style.
I was channeling Nev Nichols with the lyrical approach.
There are some sound effects at the start, bridge, and outro I like, including CB radio and some didgeridoo to give it Australian flavour.
I used to drive around a lot listening to cassette tapes of truck driving music.
I thought the future truckers might want to keep tape decks in their futuristic rigs, so there they are.
