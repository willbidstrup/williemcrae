Willie McRae... in "Brothers Of Inspiration"

We combine our skills and energy to make versions of songs which inspire us to play music.



Safe in Hornsby City (December 2024)

We went into a rehearsal room and miced up a drum kit, then did main guitar and vocals.
I spent a few hours shaping the mix and overlaying some tracks.
The whole thing was done in less than a week.

Players:

  • Phil Rodd: Drums, Doom Guitar, Lead Vocals
  • Mr Brown: Lead Guitar
  • Willie McRae: Bass Guitar, Backing Vocals

Toolkit:
  • Drums: Rehearsal Romm Default Kit
  • Guitars: Doom Guitar, Godin G Series, B.C Rich Beast
  • Amplifiers: Doom Amp, German 34 (Virtual), New York B750 (Virtual)
  • Hardware: Scarlett Focusrite Audio Interface, Rode Condenser Mic, SM58 Mics
  • Software: Cubase 12, Superior Drummer 3, AmpliTube 5, Emastered
