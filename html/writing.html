Willie McRae... Writing

Here is my official writer biography:

"Willie McRae is a South Australian writer with deep interests including jungles, volcanoes, chilli, Daleks, trucks, and roleplaying games.
He wrote his first short story at the age of eight - a work of science fiction called ‘The Alien From Yxzfexoshs.’
Since then, he has produced and distributed an assortment of words, including a collection of short stories called 'Danger Dreams'."
